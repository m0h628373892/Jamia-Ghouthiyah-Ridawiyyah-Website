Our Headteacher

A distinguished graduate of the esteemed Islamic university of Jamia Ashrafia Mubarakpur, our Headteacher has been teaching Islamic Sciences for over 25 years.

He is a recognized master of the Islamic sciences, specializing in Fiqh, Hadith, Tafsir, and Arabic Grammar. Under his guidance, JGR College maintains the highest standards of academic and spiritual excellence.
