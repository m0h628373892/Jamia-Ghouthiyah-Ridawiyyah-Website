The Legacy of Imam Ahmad Rida Khan

Our institution is strictly aligned with the doctrine of **Imam Ahmad Rida Khan** and the **Pious Predecessors**. We uphold the traditional creed of the Ahl al-Sunnah wa'l-Jama'ah, emphasizing love for the Prophet ﷺ and adherence to classical jurisprudence.

Education for Men

Full-time and part-time tracks designed to produce the next generation of Imams and community leaders.

Education for Women

Dedicated female faculty members providing a safe and empowering environment for sisters to master the sacred sciences.

A Regional Hub

JGR College is more than a school; it is a movement. Our influence stretches across the **North West**, providing guidance and scholarly support to dozens of communities.
